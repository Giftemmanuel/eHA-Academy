  1. Nigerian Coconut Rice



    2. Detail

    • duration: 45 minutes
    • servings: 4


    Ingredients Measurement
    Rice 3 cups
    Onion 1 Medium dice
    Coconut Oil/cooking Oil 4 tbs
    Garlic Mince 1 1/2 tbs
    Ginger Mince 1 tbs
    Coconut Milk 1 1/2 tbs
    Bay leaf 1
    Chicken Broth 1 Cup
    Curry 1 tbs
    Thyme 1 tbs
    Coconut Milk 1 1/2 tbs
    Scotch Bonnet 3 pieces
    Smoked Fish 1
    Grilled Chicken As prefered
    Salt As prefered
    Cubes As prefered

    Cooking Instructions

    1. Rinse the rice and leave it to drain well.
    2. Preheat two tablespoons of coconut oil, add the diced onions, and cook till soft. About 2 minutes.
    3. Add the thyme and minced ginger and garlic and cook for a few seconds. Blend the pepper roughly then add spices and seasoning, salt stir for few minutes, then add fish and chicken
    4. Stir in the rice and fry till aromatic, about 3 minutes.
    5. Add the coconut milk, broth, bay leaf and stir
    6. Cover and cook till firm to bite, about 15 minutes. Or more
    7. When is ready you can serve like that or serve with any desired protein. Happy Cooking üßë‚Äçüç≥

  2. Nigerian Curry Jollof Paster



    3. Detail

    • duration: 5 minutes
    • servings: 3


    Ingredients Measurement
    Cooked Paster 750 g
    Gino Peppe Onion Tomato Puree 150 g
    Leaves Shreded Curry As prefered
    Cinnamon Powder 4 tbs
    Onga Classic Seasoning Powder 1 tbs
    Vegetable oil 1 cksp
    Fried Fish As prefered

    Cooking Instructions

    1. Add a little water to tomato Puree,mix well to make a paste, set aside
    2. Now,heat up oil,add tomato Puree paste, cinnamon,onga Classic Seasoning Powder, stir fry on med heat, till oil floats,add washed Curry leaves
    3. Cook for 1min by stirring it in
    4. Add cooked pasta
    5. Stir in with a wooden spatula,reduce heat at this point
    6. Taste to adjust with any ingredient of choice,stir in well
    7. Cook for extra 2mins
    8. Plate and enjoy with protein of choice üßë‚Äçüç≥

  3. Nigerian Buns



    4. Detail

    • duration: 45 minutes
    • servings: 4


    Ingredients Measurement
    Flour 3 cups
    Sugar 3 1/2 table spoon
    Water As prefered
    Simas 1 1/2 tbs
    Milk 1 tin cup
    Egg 3 tbs
    Baking powder 1 tsp
    Salt pinch
    Oil for frying As prefered

    Cooking Instructions

    1. In a bowl mix flour sugar baking powder and pinch of salt
    2. Mix milk with water and add egg to it and then mix
    3. Add milk and egg mixture to the dry ingredients
    4. Mix it until it forms a dough
    5. Add butter to it
    6. Cover the mixture and leave to sit at room temperature for five minutes
    7. Preheat the oil
    8. Mix and fry in hot oil
    9. Fry until brownish
    10. Strain from oil and serve with a chilled drink
